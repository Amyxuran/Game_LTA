:She begins assembling the fragments she's gathered — Jonathan, Marco, Lily. Three people from completely different worlds, three completely different trajectories, but one common thread: they were all people crushed under extreme pressure.;

Anna:(A psychology grad student, a Wall Street trader, a top med student... all people the system ground down to the breaking point. Then the vending machine appeared, like an exit.);

Anna:(They all say they're happier now. But... Lily's eyes still held guilt. Marco's fingers hesitated when he said he didn't miss the rush. Jonathan still wakes up at 3 AM.);

Anna:(Is this happiness whole? Or did they just switch off the pain channel while the TV keeps playing?);
