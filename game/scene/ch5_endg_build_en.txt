:The next two months are the strangest of Anna's doctoral life.;

:By day she revises the IRB submission. Draft one is rejected: insufficient risk assessment. Draft two: ambiguous language in the informed-consent form. Draft three sits three weeks without an answer. At night she and Emily work over video calls, lowering the EDEN concentration again and again until the effect remains measurable but reversible.;

:One night, Anna stares at the screen and suddenly thinks the whole thing may simply never happen.;
